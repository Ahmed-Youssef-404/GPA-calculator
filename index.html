Calculate your current and cumulative GPA easily!

How to Use the Tool

  • Enter the number of courses you have registered for this semester (excluding courses not counted in the cumulative GPA).
  • Enter your current GPA.
  • Enter the number of credit hours you have completed in previous semesters (excluding the current semester).

After that, you will see two fields for each course:

  • The first to select the grade you received in this course.
  • The second to enter the credit hours for the course.

When finished, click the "Calculate GPA" button, and the following results will appear:

  • Current Semester GPA: GPA for the most recent semester.
  • New Cumulative GPA: The updated overall GPA after including this semester's results.
